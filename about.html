Overtime Pay Calculator

Know what your overtime is worth

About OvertimePayCalc.com

OvertimePayCalc.com calculates your expected weekly earnings when you work overtime. It applies the federal FLSA standard — 1.5× your hourly rate for hours beyond 40, and an optional 2× rate for double-time where it applies.

Who this is for

Hourly workers who want to verify their paycheck, salaried workers checking if they should be receiving overtime, and anyone comparing an overtime offer to a straight-time arrangement.

How it works

Enter your hourly rate, regular hours, and overtime hours. The calculator shows your regular pay, overtime pay at time-and-a-half, and total weekly earnings. Enable double-time if your state or employer requires it.
